# Banned Phrases Dictionary

Strip these from portfolio body copy. Do not replace — remove entirely or rewrite the sentence around the gap.

## Hard banned (strip on sight)

### Filler verbs and showy adjectives
- delve, delving
- showcase, showcases, showcasing
- robust
- seamless, seamlessly
- cutting-edge
- innovative
- game-changer, game-changing
- groundbreaking
- transformative (in marketing sense)

### Hedge / filler openings
- it's important to note
- it's worth mentioning
- it's worth noting
- in today's fast-paced world
- in the modern era
- in an ever-evolving landscape

### Cliché phrases
- dive into, dive deep, deep dive
- stands as a testament
- plays a vital role
- the cornerstone of
- at the heart of
- pushing the boundaries

### Connective filler (when used as paragraph openers)
- Furthermore (as a filler)
- Moreover (as a filler)
- Additionally (as filler)

### Sycophantic openers (no LLM-style preambles in portfolio copy)
- Great question!
- Absolutely!
- Certainly!

## Soft watch list (use sparingly, prefer verbs)
- passionate, passion-driven
- leverage, leveraging
- empower, empowering
- expert, expertise (use it once, prove it with outcomes)
- solutions (vague — name the specific thing)

## Punctuation rules
- **No em dashes.** Replace with commas, periods, or parentheses.
- **No emoji in body copy.** Reserve emoji for accessibility-safe icons in UI chrome only.
- **No "..."** unless it's truly mid-thought or a quote.

## Why this list exists
AI models trained on the web concentrate these phrases. Stripping them is the single highest-leverage edit for making portfolio copy sound human.
